Privacy Policy

Thank you very much for choosing Teameet, and also thank you for your trust.

You have to know that your account security and personal privacy are our top priorities.

We only collect your data during the registration, and also while you are setting your preferences. All the information will be kept in our database, and we promise that no one can retrieve your personal information from our system for illegal purposes.

During the normal operation of our website, we may collect information for the statistical analysis, including but not limited to your status, your university and your teams. All the data will not be released to the third-party organizations & companies.

As website administrators, we have the access to know your name, status and email address. This will allow us to check your identity (for instructors), and it will be convenient for us to provide any necessary help to you as well. And for sure, we will not share this information to anyone or third-party organizations & companies.

Once again, thank you very much for choosing Teameet. If you have any further questions or concerns, please feel free to contact us.
